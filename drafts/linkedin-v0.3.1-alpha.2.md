prei v0.3.1-alpha.2 is out — our first pre-release with the full deal pipeline lifecycle.

We built the screens, forms, and models to track a property from discovery (Growth Area Explorer) through 9-criteria screening, offer, due diligence, renovation, closing, and leasing. No more spreadsheets for deal workflow — it's all in one place.

Also shipping: auto-versioned Docker builds (git tag → baked metadata → structured logs), ATTOM/FRED market data integration, and growth area pagination/CSV export.

Try it: `docker compose up -d` at github.com/paruff/prei
